Why it earned its place

Some products become shorthand for an entire category. Pillow Talk is the nude lipstick — a pink-beige tuned to flatter an unusually wide range of skin tones, in a matte formula designed to feel closer to velvet than chalk. It has spawned an entire family of products, but the original bullet is still the one that made the name.

Who it’s for

Anyone who wants one lipstick that works for every occasion — the shade you buy when you don't want to think about shades.

Be honest with yourself

If you love high-shine gloss or loud color, a universal nude will bore you. This is a quiet luxury pick, not a statement piece.
